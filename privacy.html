1. Introduction

Welcome to Capsule.Works. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website and mobile application (collectively referred to as "Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2.1 Personal Information

• Account Information: When you sign up for an account, we collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information.

2.2 Usage Data

• Log Data: We collect information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and the time and date of your visit.

• App Usage: We collect data on how you use our mobile app, including features accessed and session duration.

• Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, track user preferences, and understand usage patterns.

• Tracking Technologies: We may use web beacons, pixel tags, and other tracking technologies to gather information about your interactions with our Services.

3.1 Data Usage

• Service Provision: We use your data to provide, maintain, and improve our Services, including managing your account, processing transactions, and personalizing your experience.

• Analytics: We analyze data to understand user behavior and preferences, allowing us to enhance the functionality and user experience of our Services.

• Communications: Your contact information is used to send you important notifications about your account, updates, promotional offers, and event information.

• Compliance: We may use your data to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

3.2 Data Security

• Encryption: We implement industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your personal information during transmission and storage.

• Access Control: Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job functions.

• Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and assessments to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities.

• Incident Response: In the event of a data breach, we have procedures in place to respond promptly, including notifying affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.
